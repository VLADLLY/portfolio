MY GALLERY

Welcome to my services! I offer a comprehensive range of skills including graphic design, front-end development, and proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. Whether you need captivating visuals, seamless interfaces, or polished documents, I got you covered. Let me bring your vision to life!

Black Panther

One of my earliest artworks in Photoshop was inspired by the background theme of the Black Panther movie. This piece was one of my assignments, and I created three samples for it. While this particular artwork looked good to me, I ultimately chose not to present it.

Crimson Cyphers

This artwork, created in Photoshop, was a collaborative effort, although I was the one responsible for its design within our group. I utilized the provided tools to craft it. The inspiration and theme for this project revolved around an esports team.

Ylla

This typography art was crafted by me, drawing inspiration from my girlfriend's captivating beauty. I meticulously replicated every detail from her picture. Creating this piece was a time-consuming endeavor; I dedicated a full 24 hours to its completion, foregoing sleep as our deadline approached.

WMSU PALARO background theme

This artwork was created during our Palaro event in 2023, where our theme involved taking photos and compiling them to create a unified theme. I devised an idea that centered around our Palaro event, incorporating it into my assignments. Although there were a few mistakes in the process, the overall result would have been better had I not accidentally removed the lower part of the player.

Rebranding Logo

This rebranding logo was one of our tasks in school, wherein we were tasked with remaking a logo brand and identifying its missing elements to improve upon it. My approach involved scouring Facebook for a logo brand that lacked clarity or coherence to easily identify its deficiencies.

Fallen angel

This design holds a special place among my favorites, crafted during my exploration of Photoshop. Drawing inspiration from Devil Jin from Tekken, I embarked on a search for techniques to render the fiery red hue and attach wings to the character's back. The process was time-consuming, as I was still in the learning and experimentation phase at that time.
